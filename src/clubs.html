Clubs & Societies

Explore a variety of student-run clubs that help you learn new skills, stay active, celebrate culture, and sharpen public speaking.

Technology Clubs

Hands-on groups focused on programming, robotics, AI, and product development. Members participate in workshops, hackathons, and collaborative projects to build practical skills.

  • Robotics Club — hardware + embedded systems projects
  • Programming & Web Dev — coding workshops and open-source collaboration
  • AI & Data Science Society — competitions, study-groups, and research talks

Sports Clubs

Teams and recreational groups promoting fitness, teamwork, and friendly competition across a variety of sports and activities.

  • Football & Cricket Teams — interuniversity competitions
  • Basketball & Volleyball — regular training & social matches
  • Outdoor Adventures — hiking, climbing and expedition planning

Cultural Clubs

Celebrate traditions, arts, and music. These clubs organize festivals, performances, and workshops that foster cultural exchange and creativity.

  • Music & Dance Societies — concerts, recitals, and choreography
  • Language & Heritage Clubs — events and community outreach
  • Drama & Film — theatre productions and film screenings

Debate Societies

For students who enjoy research, argumentation, and public speaking. Societies host debates, model UNs, and workshops to develop reasoning and presentation skills.

  • Competitive Debate Team — national & regional tournaments
  • Model United Nations — simulations and diplomacy training
  • Public Speaking Workshops — confidence and delivery practice

Want to join or start a club? Contact the Student Affairs office or visit the clubs portal for meeting times and membership details.
